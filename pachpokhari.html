Pachpokhari Solukhumbu Trek

About Pachpokhari Solukhumbu

Pachpokhari Solukhumbu is a breathtaking alpine destination featuring five sacred lakes. Located away from crowded routes, it offers a peaceful journey through local villages, lush forests, and incredible Himalayan viewpoints at an altitude of 4,100m.

Estimated Budget & Info

  • Transport: Kathmandu to Salleri or Phaplu by jeep/bus.
  • Food: Around Rs. 500 – Rs. 1,200 per meal depending on altitude.
  • Difficulty: Moderate trek suitable for hidden trail explorers.
  • Best Season: March–May and September–November.

5-Day Full Itinerary

  • Day 1: Travel from Kathmandu to Salleri or Phaplu
  • Day 2: Trek or drive towards Phaplu Danda / nearby village
  • Day 3: Trek to Surke or camping area near Pachpokhari trail
  • Day 4: Early morning hike to Pachpokhari and explore the lakes
  • Day 5: Return back to Phaplu or Salleri area

Note: Camping is highly recommended for the lake area.
