Pachpokhari Solukhumbu Trek

A Sacred Alpine Lake Journey in Solukhumbu

About Pachpokhari Solukhumbu

Pachpokhari Solukhumbu is a beautiful and peaceful alpine lake destination located in the Himalayan region of Nepal. The name Pachpokhari means “Five Lakes”. It is known for its natural beauty, religious importance, mountain views, and peaceful trekking experience.

This trek is suitable for travelers who want to explore hidden trails, local villages, forests, hills, viewpoints, and sacred lakes away from crowded tourist routes.

Quick Trek Information

  • Location: Solukhumbu, Nepal
  • Maximum Altitude: Around 4,100m
  • Duration: 2–4 Days from Phaplu area
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Best Season: March–May and September–November
  • Main Attraction: Sacred Five Lakes and Himalayan views
  • Starting Point: Phaplu / Salleri area

Day-by-Day Itinerary

  • Day 1: Travel from Kathmandu to Salleri or Phaplu
  • Day 2: Trek or drive towards Phaplu Danda / nearby village
  • Day 3: Trek to Surke or camping area near Pachpokhari trail
  • Day 4: Early morning hike to Pachpokhari and explore the lake
  • Day 5: Return back to Phaplu or Salleri

Lodging & Accommodation

Accommodation facilities are basic in this region. In lower areas like Salleri and Phaplu, hotels and lodges are available. Near higher trekking areas, trekkers may need basic tea houses, homestays, or camping arrangements.

Place Lodging Information
Salleri Hotels, lodges, restaurants, and basic facilities available.
Phaplu Comfortable lodges and local food options available.
Phaplu Danda Basic local stay or homestay depending on availability.
Surke Area Basic shelter or camping may be required.
Pachpokhari Remote lake area, camping is recommended.

Fooding Information

Food options are available in Salleri and Phaplu. During the trek, simple local meals are common. Trekkers should carry snacks, water, dry food, and energy items for higher sections.

  • Dal Bhat
  • Noodles
  • Fried Rice
  • Tea and Coffee
  • Local vegetables
  • Biscuits and snacks
  • Boiled water or hot lemon

Estimated Budget

  • Transport: Kathmandu to Salleri or Phaplu by jeep/bus
  • Food: Around Rs. 500 – Rs. 1200 per meal depending on location
  • Accommodation: Basic hotel, lodge, homestay, or camping
  • Guide: Recommended for remote trail safety
  • Extra Cost: Snacks, water, power bank, and emergency items

Travel Tips

  • Carry warm clothes because the lake area is cold.
  • Take raincoat and waterproof bag cover.
  • Carry enough water and snacks for remote sections.
  • Respect local culture and religious places.
  • Do not throw plastic or waste near the lake.
  • Travel with local guide if the route is unknown.
