About Trek Hunters

Adventure Begins Here

Trek Hunters is a trekking and travel platform that shares real trekking journeys, travel stories, adventure experiences, and trekking guides from Nepal.


Our mission is to inspire travelers to explore mountains, forests, lakes, high passes, and hidden trekking destinations safely and responsibly.

Featured Treks

Manaslu Circuit

Manaslu Circuit is a remote and adventurous trek around Mt. Manaslu. This route offers beautiful Himalayan views, peaceful villages, suspension bridges, forests, and a real mountain trekking experience.

Panch Pokhari

Panch Pokhari is famous for its five sacred lakes and peaceful natural beauty. The trek offers green hills, cultural villages, mountain views, and a calm environment away from busy city life.

Mardi Himal

Mardi Himal is a short and beautiful trek near the Annapurna region. It is known for close views of Machhapuchhre, peaceful forest trails, ridge walks, and stunning sunrise views.

Tilicho & Thorong-La

Tilicho and Thorong-La is a high-altitude adventure trek in the Annapurna region. The journey includes Tilicho Lake, one of the highest lakes in the world, and Thorong-La Pass with dramatic mountain landscapes.

Kang-La Pass

Kang-La Pass is a challenging trekking route in the Manang region. It offers snowy trails, prayer flags, remote villages, high mountain views, and a true Himalayan adventure experience.
