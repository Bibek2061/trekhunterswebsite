About Trek Hunters

Adventure Begins Here

Trek Hunters is a trekking and travel platform that shares real trekking journeys, travel stories, adventure experiences, and trekking guides from Nepal.


Our mission is to inspire travelers to explore mountains, forests, lakes, high passes, and hidden trekking destinations safely and responsibly.

Featured Treks

Manaslu Circuit

Manaslu Circuit is a remote and adventurous trek around Mt. Manaslu. Beautiful views and suspension bridges await.

Panch Pokhari

Famous for five sacred lakes and peaceful natural beauty. A calm environment away from city life.

Mardi Himal

A short and beautiful trek near Annapurna. Known for close views of Machhapuchhre and ridge walks.

Tilicho & Thorong-La

High-altitude adventure including Tilicho Lake and the dramatic Thorong-La Pass.

Kang-La Pass

Challenging route in Manang with snowy trails, prayer flags, and true Himalayan adventure.
