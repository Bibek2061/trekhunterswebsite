Mardi Himal Trek

About Mardi Himal Trek

Mardi Himal Trek is a gem in the Annapurna region, famous for its peaceful trails through rhododendron forests and up-close views of Machhapuchhre (Fishtail). Standing at 4,500m, the viewpoint offers a 360-degree Himalayan panorama that is perfect for both beginners and seasoned trekkers.

Quick Information & Budget

  • Location: Kaski, Annapurna Region.
  • Max Altitude: 4,500m (Mardi Himal View Point).
  • Food & Stay: Approx Rs. 3,500 - Rs. 5,000 per day.
  • Best Time: Autumn (Sept-Nov) & Spring (March-May).

6-Day Full Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive Pokhara to Kande & Trek to Forest Camp
  • Day 2: Trek from Forest Camp to Low Camp
  • Day 3: Trek from Low Camp to High Camp
  • Day 4: Sunrise hike to Mardi View Point & return to High Camp
  • Day 5: Trek down to Siding Village
  • Day 6: Drive from Siding back to Pokhara
