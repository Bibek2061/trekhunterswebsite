Mardi Himal Trek

A Beautiful Short Trek in the Annapurna Region

About Mardi Himal Trek

Mardi Himal Trek is one of the most beautiful short treks in Nepal, located in the Annapurna region. It is famous for peaceful trails, green forests, mountain views, and close views of Machhapuchhre, Annapurna South, and Hiunchuli.

This trek is perfect for beginners and travelers who want a short Himalayan adventure with amazing scenery, village life, forest trails, and sunrise views from Mardi Himal View Point.

Quick Trek Information

  • Location: Kaski, Annapurna Region, Nepal
  • Maximum Altitude: Around 4,500m at Mardi Himal View Point
  • Duration: 4–6 Days
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Best Season: March–May and September–November
  • Main Attraction: Machhapuchhre, Annapurna South, Hiunchuli, forest trail
  • Starting Point: Kande / Dhampus / Phedi from Pokhara

Day-by-Day Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Pokhara to Kande and trek to Forest Camp
  • Day 2: Trek from Forest Camp to Low Camp
  • Day 3: Trek from Low Camp to High Camp
  • Day 4: Early morning hike to Mardi Himal View Point and return to High Camp
  • Day 5: Trek down to Siding Village
  • Day 6: Drive from Siding back to Pokhara

Lodging & Accommodation

Mardi Himal trail has basic tea houses and lodges available in most stopping points. Rooms are simple but comfortable. During peak season, booking early is recommended because lodges can become full.

Place Lodging Information
Pokhara Hotels, restaurants, and full travel facilities available.
Forest Camp Basic tea houses and simple meals available.
Low Camp Lodges with mountain views and local food available.
High Camp Basic lodges available, but rooms may be limited in peak season.
Siding Village Local homestays and simple accommodation available.

Fooding Information

Food options are available in tea houses along the trail. The price increases as altitude increases because goods are carried to higher areas.

  • Dal Bhat
  • Noodles
  • Fried Rice
  • Thukpa
  • Tea and Coffee
  • Eggs and Pancakes
  • Hot lemon and boiled water

Estimated Budget

  • Transport: Pokhara to Kande and Siding to Pokhara by jeep/bus
  • Food: Around Rs. 500 – Rs. 1200 per meal depending on altitude
  • Accommodation: Tea house or basic lodge
  • Guide: Recommended for safety and route information
  • Extra Cost: Snacks, hot water, charging, Wi-Fi, and emergency items

Travel Tips

  • Carry warm clothes because High Camp and View Point are cold.
  • Start early for Mardi Himal View Point sunrise hike.
  • Carry water bottle, snacks, gloves, and raincoat.
  • Walk slowly to avoid altitude sickness.
  • Respect local culture and keep the trail clean.
  • Book rooms early during peak season.
