Kang-La Pass (5,306m)

The Hidden Gateway of Nar-Phu

Kang-La Pass connects the mystical Nar-Phu valley with the popular Annapurna Circuit. This trek takes you through remote Tibetan-style villages, ancient monasteries, and offers a breathtaking 360-degree view of the Annapurna massif.

Trek Highlights & Cost

  • Max Altitude: 5,306m (Kang-La Pass)
  • Difficulty Level: Challenging (High Altitude)
  • Best Season: Spring (March-May) & Autumn (Sept-Nov)
  • Permit: Restricted Area Permit (Nar-Phu) + ACAP
  • Estimated Budget: Rs. 55,000 - Rs. 75,000

10-Day Full Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive from Kathmandu to Dharapani
  • Day 2: Trek to Koto (Entry point for Nar-Phu)
  • Day 3: Trek from Koto to Meta
  • Day 4: Trek from Meta to Phu Village
  • Day 5: Acclimatization Day in Phu Village
  • Day 6: Trek from Phu to Nar Village
  • Day 7: Preparation day in Nar Village
  • Day 8: Cross Kang-La Pass (5,306m) to Ngawal
  • Day 9: Trek to Pisang and drive to Besisahar
  • Day 10: Return to Kathmandu
