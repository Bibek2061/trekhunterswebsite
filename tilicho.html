Tilicho Lake & Thorong-La Pass

About the Adventure

Tilicho Lake (4,919m) is one of the highest lakes in the world. Combined with the iconic Thorong-La Pass (5,416m), it is a legendary trek.

Quick Information

  • Max Altitude: 5,416m (Thorong-La Pass)
  • Difficulty: Strenuous
  • Daily Budget: Rs. 4,500 - Rs. 6,000

12-Day Full Itinerary

  • Day 1: Drive to Dharapani
  • Day 2: Trek to Chame
  • Day 3: Trek to Upper Pisang
  • Day 4: Trek to Manang
  • Day 5: Rest Day in Manang
  • Day 6: Trek to Siri Kharka
  • Day 7: Visit Tilicho Lake & Back
  • Day 8: Trek to Yak Kharka
  • Day 9: Trek to Thorong Phedi
  • Day 10: Cross Thorong-La to Muktinath
  • Day 11: Trek/Drive to Jomsom
  • Day 12: Return to Pokhara/KTM
